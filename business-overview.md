# BAU STERN ART GmbH - Business Overview

## Executive Summary

BAU STERN ART GmbH is a Hamburg-based real estate and construction company that operates as a property developer, general contractor, and real estate agency. Founded in 2018, the company has established itself as a leading developer of residential properties in and around Hamburg, with a focus on smart, high-tech, and sustainable building solutions.

## Business Activities

### Core Business Areas

BAU STERN ART GmbH operates in three main service sectors:

#### 1. Property Development (Bauträger)
The company develops new residential properties in and around Hamburg, focusing on:
- Smart building technology integration
- High-tech construction solutions
- Sustainable and environmentally conscious design
- Modern residential projects

#### 2. General Contractor Services (Generalübernehmer)
As a general contractor, the company provides:
- Complex and extensive renovation projects
- Core renovations with highest quality standards
- Coordination of building experts and specialized craftsmen
- Comprehensive project management from planning to completion

#### 3. Real Estate Agency Services (Makler)
The company acts as a broker for:
- Sales of residential and commercial properties
- Rental properties
- Property valuation services using market-standard methods and certified appraisers
- Quick sale solutions for property owners
